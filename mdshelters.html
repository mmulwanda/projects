Animal Shelters in Maryland


Tri-County Animal Shelter

Tri-County Animal Shelter (TCAS) serves the animal needs of Calvert, Charles and St. Mary's Counties. We are the only government-run shelter for the tri-county area.

Maryland SPCA

The Maryland SPCA's mission is to improve the lives of pets and people in the community. We are dedicated to helping both pets and people and to saving the lives of animals in the community.

Baltimore Humane Society

The Baltimore Humane Society was founded to protect our region's pets and to address their suffering.


SPCA-HS Spay/Neuter Clinic

The SPCA/Humane Society of Prince George's County, Inc. (PGSPCA) is a nonprofit organization of volunteers dedicated to animal welfare.

Allegany County Animal Shelter

Their services are designed to address the needs of all animals - and to provide support, education, and assistance for all the people that care about them.

Baywater Animal Rescue

We shelter and protect animals under our care, treating every life as precious. We provide foster care and training to improve adoptability with a goal of finding forever homes for the animals.


City of Greenbelt Animal Shelter

The Animal Control Division prides itself on being ethical and humane in its treatment of animals, including providing positive reinforcement training techniques.

The Humane Society of Kent County, MD Inc.

The Humane Society of Kent County Maryland, Inc is a private, charitable 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We are not affiliated with any other animal welfare organization, locally or nationally.

The Baltimore Animal Rescue and Care Shelter

We take in homeless, neglected, and unwanted animals in Baltimore City. BARCS' mission is to accept and care for all animals in need and promote responsible pet ownership for a more humane community in Baltimore City.


Defenders of Animal Rights Inc

Our shelter holds adoptable animals for as long as it takes to find them homes.

Association For Animal Rights

We are really hoping that you are an animal lover and that you might care about what happens to the cute little puppies and kittens that are outside suffering.
